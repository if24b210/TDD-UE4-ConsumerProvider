About This Project

This website is the result of a collaborative project between seven students from King Mongkut's University of Technology North Bangkok (Bangkok, Thailand) and FH Technikum Wien (Vienna, Austria).

Our goal was to create a helpful and user-friendly tool for planning outdoor activities in Vienna's beautiful parks. Whether you're looking for a playground for kids, a dog-friendly area, or parks that offer nearby necessities, our platform helps you find the best spot based on your personal needs.

How it works:

  • Simply tell us what you want to do – for example, bring your dog, find a playground, or have easy access to certain amenities.
  • We'll suggest suitable parks in Vienna that match your preferences.
  • You will then get to choose the day you want to go.
  • At the end, you'll receive a detailed summary with:
    • Information about the park you've chosen
    • Directions on how to get there
    • A checklist of things to bring with you

This project combines technology, teamwork, and a passion for making everyday life easier — and it was built across two continents!

We hope this website helps you discover new green spaces and enjoy stress-free adventures in Vienna.
